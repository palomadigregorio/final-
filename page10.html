Chapter 5

The Queen of Hearts

Alice arrives at the Queen of Hearts’ royal garden or court, after encountering a chaotic croquet game involving flamingos as mallets and hedgehogs as balls. The Queen makes a loud and dramatic entrance, surrounded by playing card soldiers. She is immediately aggressive, shouting her iconic line: “Off with her head!” at the slightest provocation. When she notices Alice, the Queen is initially intrigued but quickly turns hostile. Alice’s logical or impertinent responses often irritate the Queen, who despises being contradicted. Alice is accused of a crime (eating the Queen’s tarts), leading to a nonsensical trial.
