Chapter 9

Alice Learns Her Destiny

A scroll called the Oraculum shows the events of each day in Wonderland’s history and future. On "Frabjous Day," the prophecy foretells that Alice will slay the Jabberwocky, a monstrous dragon-like creature loyal to the Red Queen.

--

The Red Queen uses the Jabberwocky to terrorize and control Wonderland. By defeating it, Alice helps to end the Red Queen’s tyrannical rule and restore power to the kind and just White Queen.
